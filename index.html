Problems with the Current Website

Top of page

Bottom of page

  • Most users would probably be looking to find out hours, location, contact info, maybe sale info, but the first three most often
  • The most important information (hours, location, even about) isn’t readily available and it’s not clear where to find it on first inspection
  • Main letterhead and most importantly, location, have to be scrolled to
  • Scrolling action is not smooth and makes the user scroll all the way to the bottom when they might not want to do that
  • The first image isn’t the store, but prices for what are assumed to be beer the information isn’t all that helpful if you’re trying to learn about what the store is
  • Text below the letterhead, and much of the grey font is tiny, a similar color to the background, and hard to read
  • The google maps extension doesn't work, it's a nice touch, but is it really needed in this day and age where most people would just open a new tab or use their phone for directions anyway?
  • WAVE didn’t find any major accessibility issues
